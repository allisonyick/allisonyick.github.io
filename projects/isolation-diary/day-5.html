1. open the fridge

2. grab 2 eggs

3. crack the eggs in a bowl

4. add two tablespoon of milk

5. add salt and pepper to taste

6. whisk it until foamy

7. butter a pan

8. pour egg mixture into pan on low heat

9. take a spatula and slowly mix the egg so it does not stick to the bottom

10. put eggs on a plate when they are no longer runny

11. eat it
