Instructions
Submitted by Connie
1. Find a collection of books, either from your personal library, online, or an e-reader.

2. Choose which book to read by reading a synopsis of the book. This can be located on either the back or flaps of the book, or online.

3. Find a secluded, quiet, comfy place to sit or lay down. Options include a bed, a couch, or a desk.

4. Read any existing preface of the book to give yourself a better understanding of the authorâ€™s thoughts and the themes of the book

5. Go to chapter 1

6. Read the book

7. If you have not finished the book, place a bookmark in the place where you have stopped reading.

8. When you are ready to read again, repeat step 3. Then open your book to try page indicated by the bookmark, and repeat step 6 and 7. Repeat as many times as necessary, until the end of the book has been reached
