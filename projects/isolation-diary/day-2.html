Instructions
Submitted by Kayla
1. Choose 3 colors

2. Create at least 4 random blobs using each of the colors you chose on a page. Draw these blobs aligned in rows across the page

3. Using either a black or white pen/marker, add details to the blobs to create some kind of image or illustration

4. Give each blob a personality or role in relation to the others around it

5. Connect each blob by creating some kind of comic strip or story board (hence having the blobs lined up in rows)

6. Document your thing and share it! :D
